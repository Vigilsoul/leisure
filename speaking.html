Speaking

Speaking Task

Duration:
20:00
Grouping:
Pair Work

Instructions: Work in pairs and take turns having conversations based on the following prompts.

Prompt 1

I-Student A. Imagine you are a tourist visiting Costa Rica. Then, you meet a Costa Rican and start a conversation. Introduce yourself and ask for the Costa Rican’s personal information. You are on vacation, so you want to know what places you can visit in CR, what you can do here, what are good places to eat, and what are good places to get souvenirs.

I-Student B. Imagine you meet a tourist who is visiting Costa Rica and you start a conversation. Introduce yourself and ask for the tourist’s personal information. This tourist wants to know about the tourist attractions of CR, the activities that people can do, and the places that tourists can visit. Be ready to answer the tourist’s questions.

Prompt 2

II-Student A. Imagine you are on vacation and you want to go to an exciting place in Costa Rica. You are talking with a friend about this. Ask your friend about good places to visit on vacation and good activities that you can do here. With your friend, choose where to go and what to do on your vacation.

II-Student B. Imagine your friend and you are on vacation and you want to go to a nice place in Costa Rica. Give your friend ideas about where to go and what to do on your vacation in CR. With your friend, you need to choose what to do and where to go on this vacation.

Prompt 3

III- Student A. Imagine you are planning to have a trip around the world. You want to visit some of the most popular tourist attractions of the world. Talk to your friend about what countries you want to visit and what you want to do in those places. Your friend and you need to choose where to go and what to do.

III-Student B. Imagine you are planning to have trip around the world with a friend. You don’t know where to go and what to do exactly. Talk to your friend about the countries that you are interested in visiting. Also, talk about what you can do in those countries. Your friend and you need to choose what countries you want to visit and what you want to do in each of those countries.
