Warm-up

Pair Work

Instructions: Walk around the classroom get together with a classmate, spin the wheel and based on the numbers you get, ask each other the corresponding questions. Talk to as many classmates as possible. Finally, share your answers with the whole class.

  1. Who’s your favorite singer?
  2. Who’s your favorite actor?
  3. What’s your favorite song?
  4. What kind of movies do you like?
  5. How often do you go to concerts?
  6. What kind of music do you like?
  7. What’s your favorite song?
  8. What’s your favorite band?
  9. What was the last movie you saw?
  10. Did you do anything for fun last weekend?
  11. Can you play any musical instrument?
  12. Do you like dancing? Why?
  13. Do you know any song in English?
  14. What do you usually do for fun?
  15. Do you like singing?
  16. What kind of music do you hate?
  17. Can you name any bad actors?
  18. Can you name any bad singers?
  19. What’s the worst movie you saw?
  20. Who’s your favorite movie director?
