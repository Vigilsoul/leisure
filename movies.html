Movies

Let’s go to the movies!

Instructions: You want to go to the movies with some friends. Look at the movie showtimes and choose a movie you both want to see.

Useful Language

Would you like to go to a movie?

What’s playing?

How about________?

What time is it playing?

Let’s go at 8:00 p.m.

Where and what time should we meet?

Let’s meet at the mall at 7:30 p.m.

What would you like to do before / after the movie?

Why don’t we have a hamburger?

Sure! That sounds great!
